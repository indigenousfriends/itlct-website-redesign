Our Values

We believe in equality and belonging for native and indigenous communities across the world. We also believe that through these values is where we can find an authentic future with digital technology.

Humanity and Inclusion

We believe in human rights, equity, inclusion, and fostering belonging for all people to include native and indigenous communities.

Sustainability

We support long term sustainability and responsible treatment of our natural environment and its resources.

Authenticity

We believe in authentic representation and utility for indigenous communities.

Multigenerational Connection

We support multigenerational needs and beliefs that are important to indigenous or native communities.

Education

We support and encourage STEAM education where it is needed and appropriate for native and indigenous communities.

Technology and Innovation

We advocate responsible stewardship with digital tech & innovation. Technologies should not be leveraged to harm native and indigenous communities or lands.

Empowering Self-Determination

We advocate for native and Indigenous rights through digital empowerment, aligning with the UN Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples. By providing digital enablement education, programming, and advisory, we support Native and Indigenous communities to manage their cultural heritage and knowledge in ways that meet their needs and align with their values, fostering self-determination in the ever-evolving digital landscape.
